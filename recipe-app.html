Recipe App

For this project, we developed a high-fidelity prototype of a recipe app targeted at Dutch cooking enthusiasts aged 40 and above.

Together with three teammates, we focused on content, usability, and visual design. Throughout the process, we conducted thorough user research, continuously tested with end users, and implemented feedback into our design.

The app features one carefully crafted recipe – a Mexican tostada – along with custom-made content, original ingredient photography, and unique iconography.

This was my first time creating interactive prototypes in Figma, and I learned a lot about designing with users in mind, visual consistency, and turning feedback into concrete improvements. It was a valuable and inspiring experience that gave me a solid foundation in UX/UI design.
